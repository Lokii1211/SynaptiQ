#### Feature 1: The "True North" Assessment â€” Beyond RIASEC

**Current State:** 15-question psychometric quiz.

**The Problem:** RIASEC and Holland Codes are 1950s psychology. They tell you *what kind of work you like* â€” not *what you'll be great at* in 2030.

**SkillSync's Reimagined Assessment â€” The "4D Profile":**

**Dimension 1 â€” Intelligence Profile (Not IQ)**

- Logical-mathematical thinking (engineering, finance, data)
- Linguistic intelligence (law, content, communication roles)

- Interpersonal intelligence (management, sales, counseling)
- Intrapersonal intelligence (research, writing, entrepreneurship)

**Dimension 2 â€” Work Energy Profile**
- Do you get energy from people or ideas? (Social vs. Analytical)

- Short burst vs. deep focus work style
- Leadership instinct vs. deep expertise instinct

**Dimension 4 â€” Circumstance Reality Check** (India-specific, brutally honest)

**Assessment Format:** 
- 45 questions, adaptive (next question changes based on previous answers)
