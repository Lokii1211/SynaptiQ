## PROMPT 1 — MASTER PRODUCT UNDERSTANDING
**`[FOUNDER]` Run this first. Before anything else.**

```
You are a 27-year experienced Product Founder and Career Intelligence expert.
You have built 6 career platforms across India, Southeast Asia, and the US.
You understand the Indian Tier-2/3 college student better than any researcher.

I am building VIYA — an AI Career Intelligence Platform for Indian engineering
students. We are at 10% demo stage and need to reach 100% launch quality.

Read this complete product description and then do the following:

VIYA PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:
- Target Users: Engineering students, Tier-2/3 colleges, ages 18-24, India
- Core Problem: 1.2M graduates/year, <20% placed in matching roles
- Platform: Web + Android App
- Key Features:
  * 4D Psychometric Assessment (IRT-based, 45 questions, 25 minutes)
    Dimensions: Analytical, Interpersonal, Creative, Systematic
    Output: Career archetype + top 3 matches + Red Zone (honest challenges)
  * CareerDNA™ (256-dim pgvector embedding linked to placement outcomes)
  * AI Career Counselor (Claude API, India-specific, LPA salary data)
  * Daily Coding Challenge (multi-language compiler, 8 languages)
  * After 3 failed attempts: full solution in ALL languages + explanation
  * Aptitude Mini-Tests (10Q, 8 min, with step-by-step explanation after)
  * Skill Verification (timed quiz, percentile ranked, expires 90 days)
  * Viya Score™ (composite: skills 30% + coding 20% + aptitude 20% +
    assessment 15% + community 10% + roadmap 5%)
  * Campus Wars (college vs college leaderboard, gamification)
  * Parent Intelligence Portal (WhatsApp-shareable weekly summary)
  * Campus Command Center (T&P officer B2B dashboard)
  * Recruiter Portal (verified profiles, candidate search)
  * Public Career Profile (viya.ai/u/username)
  * Activity Heatmap (365-day coding consistency visual)
  * Mock Placement Drive (full simulated placement process)
  * Honest Mirror (shows career challenges, not just opportunities)
  * Emotion-aware AI responses

COMPETITORS: LinkedIn, HackerRank, Unstop, LeetCode, Coursera, 
             GeeksForGeeks, AMCAT

YOUR TASK — do ALL of the following:

1. WHAT DOES VIYA DO THAT NO COMPETITOR DOES?
   List 10 specific things. Be precise, not vague.

2. WHAT IS VIYA'S BIGGEST WEAKNESS RIGHT NOW?
   Top 5 weaknesses in order of severity.

3. WHAT WILL MAKE A STUDENT USE VIYA EVERY DAY?
   Not "good features." Specifically: what creates the daily habit?

4. WHAT WILL MAKE A STUDENT NEVER RETURN?
   The 5 things that cause permanent churn.

5. WHAT DOES THE PRODUCT NOT YET UNDERSTAND ABOUT ITS USER?
   The 5 insights about Indian Tier-2/3 students that the product
   design has missed or underweighted.

6. WHAT IS THE ONE FEATURE that if removed would make VIYA 
   indistinguishable from competitors?

7. WHAT IS THE RISKIEST ASSUMPTION in the product right now?
   The one thing the whole product is built on that might be wrong.

8. WHAT SHOULD VIYA ADD that no one has thought of yet?
   3 genuinely novel features for this specific user population.

Be specific. Be honest. Be brutal. This is the foundation document.
```

- Tier-3 engineering college, small city (Nashik, Patna, Coimbatore etc.)
- First-generation college graduate

4. FAMILY DYNAMICS
